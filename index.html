About InterHauls

InterHauls is a Nigerian Company, with world class standards aimed at delivering high quality concrete products, as well as offering haulage services to the construction industry in Nigeria. At interhauls we offer services ranging from load bearing interlocks for houses, roads and other public areas. We also have concrete roads, kerbs and concrete blocks to meet the demand of high quality contractors at competitive prices.
